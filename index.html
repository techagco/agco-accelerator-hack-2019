What's a Hackathon?

hack·a·thon   (noun) INFORMAL

A hackathon is an event that gathers the best minds in a collaborative environment with the goal of applying modern technology to solve real world issues. Specifically, we will bring to light prominent issues in the agriculture industry and hope to discover innovative tech solutions to these issues. The event runs through the course of the night to provide hackers adequate time to brainstorm, implement, and iterate on their solutions before presenting to a panel of industry professionals.

AGCO + Hack

This hackathon is open to any projects related to the world of agriculture. GSI wants to explore how modern technology can be used to solve real problems in agriculture and help our nation's farmers be more innovative and productive.
